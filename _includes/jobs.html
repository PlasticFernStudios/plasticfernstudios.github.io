Work With Us

We're always looking for interesting people to join our team, so contact us at jobs@plasticfernstudios.com with your information and resume!

Why work with us? Here's a few reasons: We value the health and happiness of our team, we value long-term job stability and growth, we get to work on interesting new games and platforms, we value learning and giving space for growth, we're a small collaborative team, we approach games holistically, and believe in fostering transparent and honest communication.

Ok sure but what about benefits? We offer health, dental, and vision insurance to all employees, we don't have core hours (so parents and others can schedule how they need to), we believe in a healthy work/life balance free from "crunch", we have regular time off throughout the year, we're a remote studio with no "home office", we believe in giving back to our communities and supporting them!
